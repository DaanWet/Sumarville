# Privacy Policy — Sumarville

**Effective date:** 14 June 2026
**Last updated:** 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data **Sumarville** ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, why we collect it, how it is used, and the rights you have under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). By adding the Bot to a Discord server or interacting with it, you acknowledge the practices described here.

---

## 1. Who is responsible for your data

The Bot is operated by an individual based in the European Union (the "Bot Owner"), who acts as the **data controller** for the personal data described below.

- **Privacy / data requests:** privacy@wettinck.be
- **General support:** [Discord support server](https://discord.gg/4Atn8t2)

> The Bot Owner's identity has been verified directly with Discord as part of the app-verification process.

---

## 2. What data we collect and why

The Bot only processes data that is necessary to provide its features (character sheets, role and nickname management, a session calendar, a food/lunch list, and NPC dialogue). All data is stored **per Discord server** and is not combined across servers.

| Data | When it is collected | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Discord User ID** | When you create a character, or when you act as a Dungeon Master | To link a character to you and to assign roles |
| **Server (Guild) ID** | When the Bot is added to a server | To keep each server's data separate |
| **Character name** | When you create a character | Stored on your character sheet; also set as your **server nickname** |
| **Character avatar / picture URL** | When you create a character | Displayed on your character sheet |
| **Configured role & channel IDs** | When a server admin runs the setup command | Bot configuration (which role is "Player"/"DM", which channels to use) |
| **Message content** | Messages you send in the Bot's command channels | To interpret and run your commands. Not retained after processing, **except** where the content itself is the feature you requested (see below) |
| **Direct message content (Dungeon Masters)** | When a Dungeon Master sends the Bot a direct message | Stored as in-game NPC dialogue for that server |
| **Feature content** | When you submit it via a command | Calendar session dates, food/lunch entries, and NPC dialogue text you choose to store |

We do **not** collect: your email address, your real name, payment information, your Discord password, or presence/activity status.

---

## 3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR Art. 6)

We process your data on the basis of:

- **Performance of a service / legitimate interest** (Art. 6(1)(b) and 6(1)(f)) — you actively choose to use the Bot's commands, and the data listed above is required to deliver the feature you requested. We only store what is necessary for that purpose (data minimisation).

You can withdraw from this processing at any time by deleting your character, asking a server admin to remove the Bot, or contacting us to request erasure (see Section 7).

---

## 4. How your data is used

Your data is used **only** to operate the Bot's features for the server you are in. We do **not**:

- sell, rent, or trade your data;
- share it with third parties for advertising or analytics;
- use it to build a profile of you across servers.

The Bot runs on Discord's platform; your use of Discord is also governed by [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

---

## 5. Where your data is stored

Data is stored on infrastructure controlled solely by the Bot Owner, located in the **European Union**. Access is restricted to the Bot Owner. No personal data is transferred outside the EU/EEA by the Bot.

---

## 6. How long we keep your data

- **Character, configuration, and feature data** is retained for as long as the Bot is present in your server.
- It is deleted when the Bot is **removed from the server**, or sooner upon a verified erasure request (Section 7).
- Erasure requests are honoured **without undue delay, and within 30 days** of verification.

---

## 7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
- **Rectification** — correct inaccurate data;
- **Erasure** — have your data deleted ("right to be forgotten");
- **Restriction / objection** — limit or object to how we process your data;
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact **privacy@wettinck.be**. To verify your identity we may ask you to confirm the request from the Discord account associated with the data. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

---

## 8. Children

The Bot is not directed at children. You must meet Discord's minimum age requirement to use Discord and the Bot (at least **13 years**, or higher where required by local law, e.g. **16** in some EU member states).

---

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Bot after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
